Bokeh FX
=================

This asset features 6 scenes of bokeh effects.

The idea of this asset was to provide an ambient animated backdrop.

Feel free to contact me if you need help with the asset or if you have problems with particle effects.

=================
Usage
=================

In contrast to my other assets, these effects are saved in the scenes rather than in prefabs. 

In the demo scenes you should find a Canvas which can be deleted to get rid of the scene selection menu in the bottom right.
